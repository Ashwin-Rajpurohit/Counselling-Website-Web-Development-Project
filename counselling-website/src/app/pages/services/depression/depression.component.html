Depression Management counselling


Depression is a common disorder or illness in the human body that affects the feelings, behavior, and act of doing the work of a particular person. In simpler terms, depression is a major depressive disorder that not only slowdowns the functioning of the human brain but also changes the physical condition of a human being.


Overview:


Depression is a neutral illness that can occur in both males and females no matter despite considering the age factor. Different types of depression have their unique causes to discuss further. In some situations, if a depression situation gets worse it can increase the chances of death. Luckily, the symptoms of depression can be easily identified such as changes in Psychological, Behavioral, and Social factors. These are the most common ways to discover depression in Men, Women, and Children. Now Depression can be cured in many ways through Depression counselling, tests, Therapy, etc.


What are the Causes of Depression?


These are some of the factors by which depression can be traced in teens, men, women:

  • Feeling Lonely
  • Loss of Interest
  • Sleeping Disorders
  • Suicidal tendency
  • Feeling Guilty

What are the symptoms of Depression?

Depression symptoms can be different for males, females, and children. And all such factors can be examined based on Behavioural Signs, Emotional Signs, and Physical Signs.


Behavioural signs:

  1. Drinking too much alcohol
  2. Avoiding family functions most of the time
  3. Doing work without the interval of proper breaks
  4. Becoming harsh in relationship

Emotional Changes:

  1. Angriness
  2. Frustration
  3. Irritated
  4. Uncomfortable

Physical Signs:

  1. Headaches
  2. Digestion problem
  3. Tiredness
  4. Sleeping Disorder
  5. Eating a lot or not
